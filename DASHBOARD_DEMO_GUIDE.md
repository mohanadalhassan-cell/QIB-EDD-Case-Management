## 🎨 COLOR CODING EXPLANATION

**Red (🔴)**
- Used for: High-risk indicators, attention-needed alerts
- Why: Universally understood as "warning" or "requires action"
- In demo: Point out that red doesn't mean "bad" — it means "monitored"

**Orange (🟠)**
- Used for: Cases needing work, investigations in progress
- Why: Middle ground between red and green
- In demo: Emphasize active workload

**Blue (🔵)**
- Used for: Processing, investigation activity, neutral status
- Why: Professional, indicates progress
- In demo: Show as "things are happening"

**Green (🟢)**
- Used for: Compliance, good metrics, closed cases
- Why: Positive, healthy, on-track
- In demo: Use as proof of system health
