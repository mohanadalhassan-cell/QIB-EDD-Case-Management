Rule CR-003: High Risk — High Risk Nationality
Created by Compliance Dept. | Effective: Feb 01, 2024
HIGH

SET COUNTRY_RISK_SCORE = MAX(COUNTRY_RISK_SCORE, 80)
IF COUNTRY_RISK_SCORE >= 80 AND ACT_RISK_SCORE >= 60 THEN FINAL_RISK_CATEG = "AUTO HIGH"
