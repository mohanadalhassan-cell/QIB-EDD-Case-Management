Governance & Approval Process

Verification, Review and Approval Steps

🏛️

Governance & Approval Workflow

All system materials, documentation, and operational procedures must follow the formal approval workflow before being used or distributed within the bank.

Approval Workflow
1️⃣ Department Preparation → 2️⃣ Compliance Review → 3️⃣ Risk Management Review → 4️⃣ Department Manager Submission → 5️⃣ Training & Development Approval
Approval Method

• Obtain approvals directly within the system via Workflow Approval.
• Or attach official approval documents inside the system as part of the case or document file.

⚖️ Compliance 🛡️ Risk Management 📚 Training & Development
⚠️

Governance Note

No material, presentation, or operational procedure may be published or used within the system unless the required approvals from Compliance, Risk Management, and Training & Development have been obtained.
