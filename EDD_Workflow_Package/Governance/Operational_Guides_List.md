## Change Management

Any updates to operational procedures follow the **Change Management Framework** with CAB (Change Advisory Board) review:

| Change Type | Approval Required |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Minor (typo, formatting) | Risk Officer |
| Procedural changes | Risk Senior Officer |
| Policy changes | Mr. Rakesh (Risk Head) |
| Regulatory-driven | Mr. Rakesh + Compliance Head |
| Architecture changes | Mr. Rakesh + IT Head |
