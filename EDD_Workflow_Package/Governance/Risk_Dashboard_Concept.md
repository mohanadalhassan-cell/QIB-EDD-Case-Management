# Risk Dashboard Concept
## EDD Digital Workflow — Qatar Islamic Bank
### March 2026

---

## Overview

The EDD Digital Workflow includes a dedicated **Risk Management Monitoring Dashboard** that provides centralized, real-time visibility over all EDD activities and risk indicators.

The dashboard is accessible to the **Risk Management Group** under the supervision of **Mr. Rakesh — Head of Risk Governance**.

## Alert Levels

| Level | Category | Response Time | Notification |
|-------|----------|---------------|--------------|
| 🔴 CRITICAL | Sanctions match, fraud, data breach | Immediate | Dashboard + SMS + Email to Mr. Rakesh |
| 🟠 HIGH | PEP unusual activity, repeated MAJOR discrepancies | 4 hours | Dashboard + Email to Risk team |
| 🟡 MEDIUM | Unusual patterns, policy changes pending > 48h | 24 hours | Dashboard + Email digest |
| 🔵 LOW | KYC expiry approaching, minor deviations | Weekly review | Dashboard only |

---

## Access Control

| Role | Access Level |
|------|-------------|
| Risk Head (Mr. Rakesh) | Full access — all panels, configuration, approvals |
| Risk Senior Officer | Monitor + Review — all panels, reports, alert acknowledgment |
| Risk Officer | Monitor — operational panels, standard reports |
| Senior Management | Executive View — summary heat map, strategic exposure |
| Auditor | Audit View — historical logs, control evidence, read-only |
| All other roles | ❌ No access — Risk Dashboard restricted |
