## 🎬 SYSTEM WALKTHROUGH TEST

**What it does**: 7-step guided tour of entire system

**How to use**:
1. Click "🎬 System Walkthrough"
2. Modal appears showing Step 1/7
3. Click "Next >" to move through steps

**The 7 Steps**:
```
Step 1 → Login Page (MFA entry point)
Step 2 → Dashboard (Daily metrics)
Step 3 → KYC (Customer verification)
Step 4 → EDD Cases (High risk cases)
Step 5 → Investigation (Analysis tools)
Step 6 → Monitoring (Real-time alerts)
Step 7 → Analytics (Executive report)
```

**Duration**: 15 minutes (full walkthrough)

## ❓ TROUBLESHOOTING

### Voice Not Working?
- ✅ Check microphone permission
- ✅ Enable speakers/headphones
- ✅ Use Chrome or Firefox
- ✅ Check system volume

### Print Button Not Opening?
- ✅ Check pop-up blocker
- ✅ Allow pop-ups for this site
- ✅ Try Ctrl+P instead

### Arabic Not Showing Correctly?
- ✅ Ensure UTF-8 encoding
- ✅ Check browser language settings
- ✅ Clear cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- ✅ Refresh page (Ctrl+F5)

### Commands Not Recognized?
- ✅ Speak clearly and slowly
- ✅ Use exact Arabic phrases
- ✅ Check microphone level
- ✅ Ensure Chrome browser

### PIN Not Working?
- ✅ Use exactly: 1234, 5678, 9012, or 3456
- ✅ Don't include spaces
- ✅ Must be 4 digits
