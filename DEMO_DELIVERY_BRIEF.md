## Answers to Common Questions

### Q: "Is this automating AML decisions?"
A: "No. The system evaluates DATA QUALITY, not customer risk. The investigator's judgment remains central. We're making sure they have reliable information."

### Q: "How is 76/100 calculated?"
A: "Each data category is verified independently. Identity gets 92% when we can confirm it three ways. Income is 60% when employment is verified but salary documentation is missing. The overall score is a composite."

### Q: "What if a customer doesn't have the documents?"
A: "The investigator decides. The system shows what the gaps are and why confidence is lower. The investigator may request documents, accept the risk, or escalate."

### Q: "Will this integrate with our actual systems?"
A: "This is a proof-of-concept with mock data. The real system will connect to MOI, DMS, payroll systems, and CRP. Same interface, real data."

### Q: "How does this compare to other EDD solutions?"
A: "Most solutions digitize the form. We've built an intelligence platform. We assess credibility. We show gaps. We help investigators decide faster and better."
